Food Gardening

Welcome to our food gardening community! Food gardening is a type of gardening that focuses on growing food crops. It is different from other types of gardening, such as ornamental gardening, in that the primary purpose of food gardening is to produce food to eat.

INTRODUCTION

  • Food Gardening Importance Community gardens provide vital access to fresh, nutritious food, especially in urban food deserts, improving food security and diet quality.
  • Food Gardening Benefits Gardening is an excellent way to get exercise and fresh air, as well as to learn about plants and their ecosystems
  • Food Gardening Health Benefits Food gardening can build your self-esteem, reduce stress and can also make you happy.
